WanderType

Read below to find out more about the various vacation personality types.

The Classics

The Tourist

Loves iconic landmarks, souvenirs, and city tours.

→
Key Traits: Curious, efficient, destination-driven
Mindset: "If I'm going to [country], I want to see what it's famous for."

Behavior:
  • Googles “Top 10 things to do in ___” and follows the list
  • Prioritizes major monuments, landmarks, and museums
  • Prefers tried-and-true itineraries with minimal surprises

The Culture-Seeker

Passionate about history, art, and local customs.

→
  • Visits museums and performances
  • Enjoys cultural immersion
  • Souvenir: Chopsticks

The Foodie

Explores new places through taste and texture.

→
  • Seeks local cuisine
  • Takes food photos
  • Souvenir: Spice or recipe

The Bold

The Adventurer

Seeks thrills, action, and adrenaline in nature.

→
  • Enjoys high-energy activities
  • Goes off the beaten path
  • Souvenir: GoPro footage

The Explorer

Fueled by curiosity and the unknown.

→
  • Prefers discovery over comfort
  • Enjoys hidden places
  • Souvenir: Field journal

The Partier

Lives for nightlife, energy, and connection.

→
  • Thrives in social scenes
  • Plans around events
  • Souvenir: Ticket stub

The Rechargers

The Relaxer

Values stillness, leisure, and slowing down.

→
  • Prefers no-rush itineraries
  • Chooses peaceful destinations
  • Souvenir: Local tea or bath salts

The Wellness Devotee

Seeks healing, mindfulness, and inner balance.

→
  • Attends retreats or spas
  • Values spiritual growth
  • Souvenir: Crystal or mantra

The Viewfinders

The Photographer

Captures the world one frame at a time.

→
  • Chases light and angles
  • Plans trips around beauty
  • Souvenir: The photo itself

The Naturalist

Feels most alive surrounded by nature.

→
  • Seeks parks, trails, ecosystems
  • Prefers quiet and connection
  • Souvenir: Pressed leaf or shell
